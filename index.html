[ 02 ] Manifesto

01

Motion is not decoration.

It's grammar. The way a thing moves tells you what it is before you read a word.

02

Systems, not screens.

We design the rules — type, colour, timing, tone — so the brand holds together everywhere it goes.

03

Weird on purpose.

Safe is forgettable. We push until it's memorable, then pull back until it's usable.

04

Ship, then sharpen.

Nothing is precious in the file. It's precious when it's live and doing its job.

Brands are verbs now.
